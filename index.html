Latitude vs. X

During this project, I analysed how weather changes the closer you to the equator. To do so, I got data from the OpenWeatherMap API to create a dataset of over 500 cities.

Afterwards, I used Matplotlib to plot weather vs latitude. The different factors I looked at included: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. On this site you will find the source data and visualisations that were made as part of the analysis.
